View full profile

I work with individuals and couples experiencing difficulties with sex, intimacy, and relationships. I offer a warm, confidential, and non-judgemental space where clients can explore sensitive concerns at their own pace and gain deeper understanding of themselves and their relationships.

I support clients who feel confused, distressed, or disconnected — whether from a partner or from their own sexuality — in making sense of emotional and relational patterns and finding ways forward that feel authentic and sustainable.

My work is inclusive and affirming, and I welcome clients from LGBTQIA+ communities, as well as those exploring questions around identity, desire, or intimacy.
I work integratively and relationally, informed by psychosexual therapy, attachment theory, and contemporary relationship therapy.

Sessions are collaborative and talking-based, with no physical contact or sexual activity involved.
